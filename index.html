Top Speed Calculator

Context:
If your 60m time is 64% or less of your total 100m time, that is considered an extremely fast start.
If your 60m time is between 64%-64.5% of you total 100m time, that is considered a fast start.
If your 60m time is 64.5%-65.5% of your total 100m time, that is considered an average start.
If your 60m time is 65.5%-66% of your total 100m time, that is considered a slow start.
If your 60m time is 66% or more of you total 100m time, that is considered an extremely slow start.
